Shashtria Pretto: A Beacon of Spiritual Light and Transformation

Shashtria Pretto, also known as Sruthi, is a luminous soul whose life embodies the essence of divine grace, unwavering faith, and profound spiritual wisdom. Born in 1974 and brought up in Mumbai, she has walked an extraordinary path shaped by celestial encounters, mystical experiences, and an unshakable connection with the Divine.

Her journey is not just one of self-discovery but also of guiding others toward healing, hope, and enlightenment. With deep humility and devotion, she has been blessed with visions of deities, saints, and celestial beings, experiences that have strengthened her resolve to spread divine wisdom and inspire others to embrace their own spiritual awakening.

A gifted healer and spiritual guide, Sruthi’s ability to channel divine energies has touched the lives of many, offering solace and transformation beyond the material realm. Her book, My Experience With The Ultimate Truth About Kundalini, is a sacred testament to her journey, offering readers a glimpse into the boundless miracles and profound truths she has witnessed.

As the chosen disciple of her Guru, who bestowed upon her the name Sruthi—meaning revelation—she continues to illuminate the world with her teachings, her humility, and her deep-seated belief in the perfection of life’s design. Through her life and writings, she serves as an unwavering beacon, guiding seekers toward their highest truth.
